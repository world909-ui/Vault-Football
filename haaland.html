Erling Haaland

Erling Haaland is one of the most exciting young talents in world football. Born on July 21, 2000, in Leeds, England, he has quickly risen to prominence as a powerful and prolific striker. Known for his incredible speed, strength, and clinical finishing, Haaland has established himself as one of the best goal scorers in the world.
