Ronaldinho

Ronaldinho, born Ronaldo de Assis Moreira, is one of football’s most creative and entertaining players. Known for his dazzling dribbling, free kicks, and joyful playing style, Ronaldinho brought flair and artistry to the sport like few others. He won the FIFA World Cup with Brazil in 2002 and the Ballon d'Or in 2005. His time at FC Barcelona marked a golden era, where he became a global icon and fan favorite. Beyond the titles, Ronaldinho’s legacy lies in the way he played—with a smile, for the love of the game.
